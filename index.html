How to use:

  1. Click on the "Add task here" container and write in your task
  2. Press the "Add" button or the "Enter" key to add the task to the list
  3. Press the "✅" button to mark the task as done
  4. Press the "❌" button to delete the task
  5. Press the "🔴" button to mark the task as high priority
  6. Press the "🔵" button to mark the task as medium priority
  7. Press the "🟢" button to mark the task as low priority
  8. (Optional) Add a status for your task from the provided statuses
  9. (Optional) Add a specific date or deadline for your task
  10. Press the "📝" button to edit your task
  11. While in the edit function press the "Save" button or the "Enter" key to save your changes
